# Visualization Versus Textual

In order to prove the effectiveness of VPCL, an empirical study was conducted.

## Study Methodology

Participants: 30 students at the State University of New York at Old Westbury who had knowledge of the C programming language

Groups: Divided into two groups of 15
- VPCL group: Exposed to VPCL
- Textual group: Not exposed to VPCL

Tasks: Three different sorting algorithms (Exchange, Selection, and ExSel)

## Results

Selection Sort:
- VPCL Group: 13 correct
- Textual Group: 9 correct

Exchange Sort:
- VPCL Group: 11 correct
- Textual Group: 7 correct

ExSel Sort:
- VPCL Group: 10 correct
- Textual Group: 6 correct

## Conclusion

These results suggest the relative superiority of VPCL over conventional programming methods.
